How to Use the Application

Using Medai is simple and user-friendly.To get started, the user just needs to enter their symptoms into the provided input area on the chat interface. Once submitted, the bot will instantly respond with relevant precautions, medicine recommendations, and health tips based on the symptoms entered.

To try the application, simply click on the "Try medai" button below.

AI-Powered Health Assistant

Medic Bot is a web-based health assistant application designed to provide users with instant medical guidance based on their symptoms. The core functionality revolves around delivering precautionary measures, recommended over-the-counter medicines, and health tips, ensuring users receive timely and relevant support without needing immediate clinical visits.

The application uses a symptom-driven approach, where users input a list of their symptoms into a simple, intuitive chat interface. The bot processes this information and returns helpful suggestions tailored to the symptoms, enhancing user awareness and decision-making in early-stage healthcare.
